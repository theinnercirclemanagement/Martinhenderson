A Morning in Virgin River

June 10, 2025

Woke up before dawn today. The set was quiet — just the sound of the river and birds.

I sat on the porch of Jack’s cabin with a coffee, wearing his jacket, thinking about where he’s been. Season 7 is digging deeper into his past — not just the military, but what it means to come home.

The crew wasn’t filming yet. Just me, the mist, and the trees.

I recorded a short voice note this morning. It’s attached below — just the sounds of the river and a few thoughts. Nothing polished. Just real.
